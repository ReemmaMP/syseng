Testing strategies

Along with researching about how to build the app, we looked into Unit testing as well as this is essential to ensuring that our code works in every possible scenario that can be generated. According to this article*, our unit test should be equipped to do the following:

  • Set the conditions (pre-conditions)
  • Cause the trigger
  • Check for the expected results

Due to the time limit on this project, we may not be able to explore all the different options for testing. However, a few may be more relevant and good to have implemented. We will be focusing on testing for most commonly occurring issues. For example, we may find that boundary conditions* are a prime source of the bugs found in our application code.
